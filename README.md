## 📊 Key Findings & Business Insights

<img width="900" height="720" alt="dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5173cdcb-b703-4bfc-a311-4b04754358d9" />

### 🔹 1. Category Performance

* Technology and Office Supplies demonstrate **strong profit margins**
* Furniture generates **high revenue but comparatively low profit**
* Indicates potential issues in **pricing strategy or cost structure**

👉 **Recommendation:** Re-evaluate pricing and cost optimization for Furniture

---

### 🔹 2. Fake Growth Identified

* Certain sub-categories (e.g., Tables) exhibit:

  * High sales volume
  * Negative profitability

👉 This reflects **revenue growth without sustainable profit**

👉 **Recommendation:** Reduce discounting or reassess product positioning

---

### 🔹 3. Discount Impact on Profitability

* Clear negative correlation observed:

  * Higher discounts → Lower average profit
  * Deep discounts frequently result in losses

👉 **Recommendation:** Implement discount thresholds (e.g., cap at 20–30%)

---

### 🔹 4. Customer Contribution (Pareto Analysis)

* Top ~20% of customers contribute a **majority of total revenue**

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Prioritize retention strategies for high-value customers
* Introduce loyalty and personalized engagement programs

---

### 🔹 5. Customer Retention Analysis

* A significant proportion of customers are **one-time buyers**

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Improve retention via targeted offers, follow-ups, and personalization

---

### 🔹 6. Regional Performance

* Certain regions consistently show **low or negative profitability**

👉 Possible drivers:

* High shipping costs
* Excessive discounting
* Suboptimal pricing

👉 **Recommendation:** Implement region-specific pricing and cost strategies

---

### 🔹 7. Delivery Efficiency

* Delivery timelines vary significantly across regions
* Some regions experience **notable delays**

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Optimize logistics and supply chain operations
* Improve warehouse and distribution efficiency

---

### 🔹 8. Loss-Making Sub-Categories

* Several sub-categories consistently operate at a loss

👉 **Recommendation options:**

* Reprice products
* Reduce discount dependency
* Optimize sourcing and supply chain
* Consider discontinuation if necessary

---

### 🔹 9. Discount Dependency

* Certain categories rely heavily on discounts to drive sales

👉 This indicates an **unsustainable revenue model**

👉 **Recommendation:** Shift towards value-based pricing strategies

---

### 🔹 10. Risky Transactions

* Frequent occurrence of orders with:

  * High discounts
  * Negative profit

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Introduce business rules to prevent unprofitable transactions

---

### 🔹 11. Sales Seasonality

* Sales exhibit peaks during specific periods (commonly Q4 in retail)

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Align inventory planning with demand cycles
* Optimize marketing campaigns for peak seasons

---

### 🔹 12. Profit Trend Over Time

* Cumulative profit shows overall growth with fluctuations

👉 Indicates:

* Inconsistent performance
* Potential seasonal or external influences

---

### 🔹 13. High-Value Customers (Profit-Based)

* Some customers generate **high profit despite moderate sales volume**

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Prioritize these customers over high-revenue but low-margin ones

---

### 🔹 14. Segment Analysis

* Consumer segment dominates overall sales
* Corporate and Home Office segments often yield **better margins**

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Focus growth strategies on high-margin segments

---

### 🔹 15. Basket Size Behavior

* Orders containing multiple products indicate:

  * Strong cross-selling potential

👉 **Recommendation:**

* Implement product bundling strategies
* Use recommendation systems to increase basket size
